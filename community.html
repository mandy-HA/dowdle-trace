About Dowdle Trace

Dowdle Trace is a private residential community nestled in the Blue Ridge Mountains of western North Carolina, just outside the charming town of Franklin. Our neighborhood is known for its stunning mountain scenery, active social calendar, and the kind of neighborly spirit that's increasingly rare to find. Whether you've lived here for decades or just arrived, you'll find a community that genuinely looks out for one another.

Life in Franklin

Franklin, NC offers a wonderful quality of life — a vibrant small-town downtown, excellent hiking and outdoor recreation, and four distinct seasons all framed by the Nantahala National Forest. Macon County is a gem of western North Carolina, and Dowdle Trace puts you right in the heart of it.

Community spirit

From the annual Love Your Neighbor gathering to Ladies' Lunch and summer cookouts, Dowdle Trace has a full social calendar. Our residents are active, engaged, and genuinely invested in making this a great place to live. New neighbors are always warmly welcomed.
